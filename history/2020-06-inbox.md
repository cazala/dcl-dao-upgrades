# 2020-06 addition of community inbox

An inbox flow for community-sourced initiatives was added as an additional step ahead of a larger community vote for enacting actions.

The inbox required less voting participation/quorum but added a second 24hr delay step before launching a wider community vote to enact the proposed action.

This change was enacted over two steps:

1. Adding a new inbox voting aggregator (sourcing from the community voting aggregator) to power a new inbox voting mechanism. See the related [repo](https://github.com/sohkai/sohkai-dcl-app-install-2020-05/tree/mainnet).
2. A delay app (24hr) was added between the inbox voting app and community voting app. See the related [gist](https://gist.github.com/sohkai/6a9334bba8c06117f30b71dfe3a5d7d0#file-dcl_delay-js).
